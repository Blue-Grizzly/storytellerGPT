StorytellerGPT

This Open AI project uses the chatgpt API to generate an interactive story in a setting of your choice.


Before you start:

  • To get started you first have to provide a setting for your story to take place.
  • You also have to tell it what you want your character to be.
  • You will not be able to change these after starting without starting a new story
  • For the best experince write your actions in first person.
  • Be short and concise.
